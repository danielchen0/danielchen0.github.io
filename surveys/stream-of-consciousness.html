When did you first start having a continuous stream of consciousness?

By "stream of consciousness," I mean an ongoing internal monologue or narration of your thoughts — a sense that you are continuously thinking in words or images. Some people recall this beginning in early childhood; others feel it emerged later. There is no right or wrong answer.
